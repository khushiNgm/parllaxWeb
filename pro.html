There are probably as many definitions for AI as there are areas within AI and the
numbers keep growing.However, I am including a few below. AI refers to “robots, computers, and other
machines with a humanlike ability to reason and solve problems” (McPherson, 2018, p. 4). Artificial
intelligence is the theory and developmet of computer systems that are able to perform tasks that normal
require human intelligence, such as visual perception,speech recognition, decision-making,
and translation between languages. Artificial intelligence makes it possible for machines to learn from
experience, adjust to new inputs, and perform human-like tasks.
